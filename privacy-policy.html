The Budgeting Fix

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to The Budgeting Fix. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Information stored in the app

The only information stored in the app is what comes from your imported bank statement CSV and what you manually enter yourself. This includes:

  • Transactions — descriptions, amounts, dates, and auto-assigned categories imported from your bank statement CSV
  • Savings goals — goal name, target amount, and monthly contribution you type in manually
  • Budget limits — spending limits you set manually for each category
  • Recurring transactions — subscriptions and bills either added manually or auto-detected from your imported CSV

Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or personal identity
  • We do not collect your bank account numbers or login credentials
  • We do not have access to your actual bank accounts
  • We do not track your location

3. How Your Data is Stored

All your financial data is stored locally on your device only. We do not upload, transmit, or store your personal financial information on any server. Your data stays on your phone.

4. Bank Statement Import

When you import a bank statement CSV file:

  • The file is read and processed entirely on your device
  • No data from your bank statement is ever uploaded to our servers
  • No data from your bank statement is shared with any third party
  • You can delete all imported data at any time from Settings

5. Pro Subscription Payments

If you upgrade to The Budgeting Fix Pro:

  • Payments are processed securely by Stripe
  • We never see or store your credit card information
  • Stripe handles all payment data under their own Privacy Policy
  • Your subscription status is stored locally on your device

6. Third Party Services

We use the following third party services:

  • Stripe — for processing Pro subscription payments (stripe.com/privacy)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — for app distribution and in-app purchases

7. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • All your data is stored locally on your device
  • Payment processing uses industry standard encryption via Stripe
  • We never expose API keys or sensitive credentials in the app

8. Age Requirement

The Budgeting Fix is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you are under 18 please do not use this app.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all your data at any time (Settings → Clear All Data)
  • Cancel your Pro subscription at any time
  • Stop using the app at any time

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.
